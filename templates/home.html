Welcome to An Intelligent System

Advanced predictive tool designed to assess the severity and progression of diabetic retinopathy in individuals through automated analysis of retinal images.

Diabetic Retinopathy (DR) is a diabetes-related eye condition that damages the blood vessels in the retina, potentially leading to vision loss if untreated.


  • No_DR – No visible signs of retinopathy.
  • Mild DR – Early signs such as microaneurysms.
  • Moderate DR – Increased number of microaneurysms and hemorrhages.
  • Severe DR – Blocked blood vessels deprive the retina of oxygen.
  • Proliferative_DR – Advanced stage where abnormal blood vessels grow.
